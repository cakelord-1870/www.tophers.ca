Description

This bar is infused with a blend of Kaolin and Bentonite Clay. The swirl of the bar is further infused with Activated Bamboo Charcoal to help detoxify and remove excess oils from your skin without being drying. The fresh + clove free Bay Rum scent is reminiscent of barber shops of old and is truly masculine. Bay Laurel, Patchouli and Mixed Citrus, need I say more.

This long lasting, silky bar is full of plant based oils and butters that gently clean, are never drying and great for skin conditioning and health while the clay in each bar helps to pull impurities and extra oils to the surface to be rinsed away with the creamy lather.  We cut each at roughly 4.5 - 5 oz bars.

We make our soaps the old fashioned way in small batches to control quality. These palm oil and cruelty free soap bars contain no palm oil, lard or tallow and are never tested on animals, only on me.

Our cold process, small batch soap is made using a combination of Coconut oil, Shea butter, Sunflower oil, Canola oil, Olive oil, Castor oil, Sodium Hydroxide(Lye), Select Clays, Essential oils and/or phthalate free Fragrance oil. No harsh chemical detergents.

They are further cured for 4-6 weeks to fully saponify and harden giving you a long lasting, great smelling bar of real soap.

Test on skin before use, may contain allergens.

Due to the handmade nature of this soap color variation may differ slightly from photos. Allowing your soap to dry between uses will lengthen the life of your bar.
