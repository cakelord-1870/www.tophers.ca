Description

Soaking in a bath is beneficial in many ways,not to mention relaxing + warm in the cold winter months.  The added benefits of Eucalyptus Essential Oil will aid in removing toxins from both the air and body.  If you are feeling under the weather, it's also a great decongestant.

The waters of the Dead Sea have been renowned for their therapeutic effects since ancient times. Two thousand years ago, Flavius wrote that the salts from the Dead Sea heal the human body.

The waters from the Dead Sea are unique in that they contain 27% of various salts as compared to 3% in normal sea water. Further, while sodium accounts for approximately 80% of the salt content of normal sea water, it comprises much less of the salt total in water from the Dead Sea. The balance of the salts in Dead Sea water are magnesium, potassium, calcium chloride and bromides.

Give yourself a spa like treatment in your own bathtub. A natural way to help your largest organ (the skin) begin the process of cleansing and renewal! Detoxifying Dead Sea Mineral Salts with Kaolin Clay are combined with pure essential oils to draw impurities from your skin while you soak in a relaxing bath. Using 100% natural Lavender, Cedarwood and Vetiver Essential Oils, this exotic Dead Sea bath salt helps to promote a peaceful and relaxed state to help you drift off to sleep.

Stir 2 - 4 tablespoons into a tub of warm water until in dissolves. Soak for 20 minutes, immersing your body, keeping the water at a consistent temperature and relax while the Dead Sea Salt minerals and the infused essential oils go to work.

Ingredients: Dead Sea Salts, Kaolin Clay, Eucalyptus Essential Oil,  Polysorbate 80, Soap Colorant.

Test on skin before use, may contain allergens.
