Bath Bombs, Bubbles + Shower Steamers

All the Bath Treats you could possibly need will be found here.  Over 2 dozen scents of Bath Bombs, Bubble Bars + Shower Steamers for you to choose from.  Making bath time fun is our favourite thing to do.

Topher's regular sized Bath Bombs are about 2.75  in size and made with all top quality ingredients.  Our large Bombs weigh in at roughly 6.5 oz.  All our bombs will tint your water while surrounding you with our amazing fragrances. 

Our bubble bars are a form of solid bubble bath + will fill your tub with thick, luxurious + long lasting bubbles.

Shower Steamers are for those you don't use the bath tub + prefer a shower but would like the aromatherapy of a bath bomb.  Just throw in the corner of the shower out of direct spray + enjoy the experience.
