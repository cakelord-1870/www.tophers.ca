Description

Calm the senses with our rich spiced floral accord. French lavender is warmed with spicy accents of nutmeg and ginger for the main, a background of sweet vanilla bean dances with the florals, adding a lingering sensation of comfort.

6 cubes - 3 oz - 85 g - Bigger than "the other guys"

We load our melts with the maximum allowable amount of fragrance as we want our melts to last longer and travel further.  Each 1 inch cube offers between 15 to 20 hours of room filling fragrance, giving you between 80 to 100 hours per 3 oz package.

Our melts are made from 100% soy wax and our added pthlalate free fragrance.  No other additives.  Soy wax is a complete vegetable wax made from soy beans, a renewable and biodegradable resource so it's better for the environment than some other petroleum based waxes or parasoy blends.  The wax is cleaner and less likely to bother anyone with allergies.  Made with no genetically modified materials, and the packaging is #3 recyclable plastic.

Ingredients - 100% Soy Wax, Fragrance.
